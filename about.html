Our Story

Hi, I'm Ramy Amer

Content Creator & Founder

As a passionate content creator, I know exactly how exhausting it can be to manage a consistent posting schedule. There were days when I found myself constantly checking the clock, pausing my actual creative work, or waking up at odd hours just to manually publish a video on TikTok at the "perfect" time to reach my audience.

I searched for a scheduling solution that was simple, reliable, and didn't require a steep learning curve, but I couldn't find exactly what I needed. So, I decided to build it myself.

What started as a personal internal tool to solve my own scheduling nightmare quickly proved to be incredibly powerful. I realized that thousands of other creators, social media managers, and digital agencies were facing the exact same struggle. They needed a tool that was easy to use, highly valuable, and focused purely on getting the job done efficiently.

That’s how AutoSharVid was born. My mission is to give you your time back. By automating the tedious part of content management, you can focus your energy on what you do best: creating amazing content.

Let's grow together.
