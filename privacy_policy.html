Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

AutoSharVid ("we", "our", or "us") operates this tool to automate video publishing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with TikTok through our tool, we receive an authorized access token. We DO NOT collect, store, or process your personal data, passwords, or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

The TikTok access token is used strictly and exclusively for uploading and publishing videos directly to your TikTok account on your behalf, utilizing the TikTok Content Posting API.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We deeply respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any outside parties. We implement strict security measures in our automated workflows to protect your access tokens. Tokens are securely processed and never exposed publicly.
